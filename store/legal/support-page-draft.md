## Common Questions

### What does OpenFit do?

OpenFit shows Google Health data in a configurable dashboard with activity rings, metric cards, and home-screen widgets.

### Does OpenFit provide medical advice?

No. OpenFit is a personal wellness utility and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

### What data does OpenFit access?

OpenFit accesses the Google Health data you authorize through Google sign-in. On iOS, it can write supported weight, sleep, and workout records to Apple Health when you start the export and grant HealthKit permission.

### How do I disconnect Google?

Sign out in OpenFit and revoke OpenFit access from your Google account permissions page.

### How do I revoke Apple Health access?

Open the iOS Health app or iOS Settings, find OpenFit under Health permissions, and turn off access.
